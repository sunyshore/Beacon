Mental Health Resources

Here are some of the mental health resources and services that Queen's University offers.

Student Wellness Services (SWS)
Mitchell Hall First Floor
613-533-2506: Call between 8:00 am and 4:30pm, Monday through Friday to book an appointment.

Queenâ€™s Student Accessibility Services (QSAS)
QSAS seeks to support and empower students experiencing disability related barriers to access and fully participate in their own educational experience. QSAS provides educational support to students based on their first-person experience of disability and supporting documentation. Students seeking academic accommodations should complete the process outlined here.

Therapy Assistance Online (TAO)
TAO is an online, mobile friendly library of engaging, interactive pathways that promote wellness and can help you bounce back from challenging times.

Peer Support Centre (PSC)
JDUC Room 26, Currently Online
The Peer Support Center provides undergraduate students with confidential, empathetic, peer-to-peer support including resource referrals and practical guidance.

Empower Me
1-833-628-5589
Call 24/7 from anywhere in North America
Visit the linked website for toll free numbers outside North America and more information
Empower Me offers Equity, Diversity, and Inclusivity sensitive service. Students can request culture, race, religion, gender identity/sexual orientation specific support in multiple languages.

Good2Talk
1-866-925-5454 or text GOOD2TALKON to 686868 (Ontario)
1-833-292-3698 or text GOOD2TALKNS to 686868 (Nova Scotia)
Good2Talk provides confidential support services for post-secondary students in Ontario and Nova Scotia.
